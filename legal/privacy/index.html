Privacy Policy

Virelian respects the privacy of website visitors and registry applicants.

Information Collection

Applicants provide personal and professional information necessary for identity verification and standards review. Registry listings include name, credential status, and issuance date.

Data Use

Personal information is used solely for credentialing administration, verification processes, and institutional communications.

Data Security

Virelian employs industry-standard security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

User Rights

Credential holders may request access to their information, request corrections, or withdraw from the registry by contacting Virelian.
