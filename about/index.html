INSTITUTIONAL OVERVIEW

About Virelian

Virelian is an independent credentialing authority focused on verification, ethics, and public accountability in modern journalism.

Purpose

Virelian exists to strengthen public trust in independent journalism by establishing clear standards, verification pathways, and transparent governance.

What We Do

  • Maintain published professional standards and ethics guidance
  • Operate a public-facing registry for verified contributors
  • Publish research briefings and institutional updates
  • Support credential renewal through periodic review

What We Are Not

Virelian is not a newsroom, not a talent agency, and not an advocacy organization. We do not sell influence, placement, or access.
