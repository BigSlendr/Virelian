APPLICATIONS

Apply for Verification

Verification is designed for independent journalists, contributors, and investigators seeking a public record of credentialed standing. Applications are reviewed for identity, professional context, and standards alignment.

Application Overview

Applicants should provide verifiable identity documentation, a clear professional role, and representative work samples. Outcomes include approval with registry listing, conditional approval with additional review, or deferred status pending documentation.

Submit via Email

  • Documentation
