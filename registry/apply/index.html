Apply for Verification

Submit a verification request with clear documentation and professional context.

Eligibility

Applicants must demonstrate independent journalism activity, adherence to professional ethics, and a verifiable body of work.

Required Materials

  • Proof of identity and location.
  • Portfolio links to recent work.
  • Professional role and primary beat.
  • Statement of editorial standards and accountability.

Review Process

Submissions are reviewed for documentation completeness, ethics alignment, and professional history. Additional verification may be requested.

Timelines

Initial reviews are typically completed within 30 business days.
