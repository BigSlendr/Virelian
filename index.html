How Verification Works

  1. Identity Confirmation

    Applicants provide documentation to establish authentic identity and professional context.

  2. Professional Context Review

    Standards committee evaluates work history, ethics alignment, and public accountability.

  3. Credential Issuance & Registry Entry

    Approved contributors receive registry inclusion subject to periodic review and renewal.
