Governance

Institutional Governance

Institutional credibility requires structure. Virelian publishes governance documentation to support transparency and accountability.

Oversight Model

Decision-making authority is distributed across standards review, credential administration, and institutional communications to prevent concentration of influence and ensure procedural integrity.

Standards Committee

Committee members are selected for professional experience, ethical judgment, and commitment to transparency. Standards decisions are documented and subject to institutional review.

Disputes & Review

Contributors may request formal review for suspensions or revocations. Reviews are conducted by an independent panel with documented outcomes.

Documentation

  • Charter (Foundational) — Mission, independence principles, and governance structure.
  • Ethics Review Process — Procedures for standards violations and dispute resolution.
  • Registry Criteria Overview — Verification requirements and renewal pathways.
